Elevator Use Algoritm

- Find elevator buttons
- Take input on if you want to go up or down
	- Press the desired button
-Wait until the elevator arrives and opens
-Enter the elevator
-Locate the elevator control panel 
-Take input for what floor you want to go to
	-Press the button for the desired floor
-Wait for doors to close
-Monitor the current floor
	-When current floor equals desired floor wait for door to open
	-When door is open walk out of the elevator and onto the desired floor
