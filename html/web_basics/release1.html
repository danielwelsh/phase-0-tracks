I Love Fast Cars!

Car companies continue to push boundaries and make faster and faster street legal cars. Hybrid technology has meant cars now utilize the massive torque of electric motors to drop 0-60 times significantly.

A list of hybrid combustion-electic hyper cars:

  • Ferrari LaFerrari
  • Mclaren P1
  • Porsche 918

These three cars are in competition to determine who has the fastest 0-60 time.

  1. Porsche 918 Spyder: 2.3s
  2. Ferrari LaFerrari: 2.6s
  3. Mclaren P1: 2.7s

The Porsche 918 is very impressive and is the fastest of all the hyper cars.
